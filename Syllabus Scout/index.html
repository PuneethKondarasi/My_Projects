Syllabus Scout

What is Syllabus Scout?

Syllabus Scout is a web application that allows students to search for study materials for a given topic or syllabus. This can be useful for students who are looking to get a head start on their classes by providing them with all the study materials that they require in a single place.

How does it work?

Students can search for study material by entering the name of the topic or by uploading the syllabus. The application will then return a list of study materials that match the search criteria. Students can then click on the material to view it.

FAQ's

1. Is the Basic Plan really free?

Yes, absolutely! Our Basic Plan provides free access to a limited selection of study materials. It's a great way to explore our platform and see what we have to offer.

2. What payment methods do you accept for the Premium Plan?

We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also support payments through PayPal for your convenience.

3. Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Of course! You can cancel your subscription to the Premium Plan at any time, no questions asked. Your access will continue until the end of your billing cycle.

4. Is there a limit to how many study materials I can access?

No, there is no limit to the number of study materials you can access with the Premium Plan. You'll have unlimited access to our entire library of study materials for the duration of your subscription.

5. How can I get help if I have a question or encounter an issue?

If you have any questions or encounter any issues while using our platform, our support team is here to help. You can reach out to us
